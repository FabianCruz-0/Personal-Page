Welcome, Im Fabián Eduardo Cruz Amador.

At the time that im writing this, im coursing a Software Engieering degree in Facultad de Ingeniería Mecánica y Eléctrica, in Nuevo León, México.


I'm a full stack Developer, focusing the Web Develpment, but I also have programming skills with Java, C and C#. I have learn my skills self-taught, doing research and doing Projects.

Nowadays I'm very interested in complete Web Development and Android Native and hybrid development, but im in the way to learn more.
